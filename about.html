About Midnight Oil

Curiosity first.
Conclusions earned.

Midnight Oil Entertainment is built around exploring strange places, challenging familiar stories, and documenting what actually happens.

We investigate haunted locations, cryptid legends, unexplained events, local folklore, historical mysteries, and the stories that refuse to disappear.

We take the history seriously, keep the investigation honest, and leave room for the weird, funny, and completely unexpected moments that happen along the way.

Our approach

Investigate first. Decide later.

We aren't interested in forcing every strange event into a paranormal explanation—or dismissing something simply because it sounds impossible.

01

Research

We start with the history, folklore, witnesses, and documented claims surrounding each case.

02

Explore

Whenever possible, we go directly to the locations connected to the story.

03

Document

We record what happens, examine what we capture, and separate evidence from assumption.

04

Stay curious

Sometimes the answer is paranormal. Sometimes it's ordinary. Sometimes there isn't an answer at all.
